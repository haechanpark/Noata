About the Project

Utilizing the data from MIDI files, this program can use big data analysis to collect all commonalities between pieces of music, recordings, and more! The device will also be able to accurately recognize the composer of a piece if the information is given and that same data will be compiled and presented to the user.

Data like this can be utilized to help composers and song writers to create pieces of music that contains motifs and lyrical rythms from their favorite musicians.
